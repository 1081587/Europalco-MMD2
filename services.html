EUROPALCO did not expect the events in 2020 to be so different from the events of previous years. However, the secret lies in how we deal with adversities and in the circumstances in which we live it is necessary to adapt in order to continue providing the best solutions to our clients.

Therefore, at EUROPALCO we decided to create the "Studio" so that our customers can continue to hold their events in digital format. The truth is that in the face of the COVID-19 pandemic, companies have been forced to adapt to this new reality and therefore EUROPALCO proposes this differentiating solution so that our customers can continue to hold successful events.
