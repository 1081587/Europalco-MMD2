STUDIO

The gateway to digital events

EUROPALCO did not expect the events in 2020 to be so different from the events of previous years. However, the secret lies in how we deal with adversities and in the circumstances in which we live it is necessary to adapt in order to continue providing the best solutions to our clients.

Therefore, at EUROPALCO we decided to create the "Studio" so that our customers can continue to hold their events in digital format. The truth is that in the face of the COVID-19 pandemic, companies have been forced to adapt to this new reality and therefore EUROPALCO proposes this differentiating solution so that our customers can continue to hold successful events.

EUROPALCO is always up to date with new event trends and therefore we know that at this stage Live streaming is a tool with great potential for digital events. In addition, it is possible to interact with participants online via any device and in multiple languages through online simultaneous translation.

The Set

  • 2 recording studios with 168m 2 with chroma key ;
  • 3 Chromas (possibility to divide up to 6 of 7x6m);
  • 1 6x3m + 3x3m + 5x3m Ledwall;
  • 1 Streaming system with the capacity to do up to 12 streams simultaneously;
  • 1 Class: Virtual Studios and Augmented Reality; Telepoint;
  • Construction of bespoke scenarios, virtual and 3D scenarios;
